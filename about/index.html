Since 2007 I've been in the GIS field professionally and for the past nine plus years I have been working as a remote GIS Consultant supporting an array of clientele. Having gained exposure to GIS while at Penn State University and interning with the National Park Service, I landed a typical entry level GIS role with an environmental evaluation company. I earned the opportunity to fill their GIS Specialist position when it became vacant, having demonstrated my capabilities, where I supported the company's Aerial Imagery, Fire Insurance, Geocoding and Topo Departments. In 2013 they were acquired by a large corporation setting up the perfect opportunity to branch out on my own where I'd be able to consult both on-site as well as remotely.

Which brings us to today where I continue serving as a seamless extension to GIS departments needing just that extra little bit of help and/or guidance. At this point in my career there isn't much that I can't problem solve or reverse engineer and the people I've worked with are at ease tossing wide ranging inquiries my direction. I'm equally adept dealing with raster, vector, mesh and point cloud input of all magnitudes that's either been provided or that I've had to resource myself. Sometimes that means scraping and batch processing hundreds of image tiles using a customized script, retrieving and reformatting geojson data from an API for other analysts to use or grabbing elements from an interactive map retaining pertinent attributes. I would love to have a hand in helping your vision fulfill its potential. Feel free to reach out if you need:

  • Data Engineering - You've colleced a variety of data from multiple sources, need an assist getting them to play nicely with each other?
  • Route Optimization - Have a set of customers you need to reach regularly and would like to optimally schedule based on location?
  • Site Selection - Do you have a set of criteria that you'd like to use to select a new location?
  • Viewshed Analysis/Line of Sight - Are you deciding where to place an antenna and need help visualizing its reach?
  • Asset Management - Do you have a space with a large footprint and you need to keep track of where elements are arranged?
  • Data Acquisition - Need help tracking down information?
  • Image Classification - Are you flying a drone and have a raster (image) that needs to be evaluated?
  • Process Automation - Tired of wasting energy on tedious tasks?
  • Custom Maps - Is there a special event or location you need would love to see memorialized?
